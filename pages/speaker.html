A four-day company visit, activities, lectures, and workshops

Meet Our Speakers:

Mr. Arnel Gonzales

During our tour at the PAGASA station, Mr. Arnel Gonzales provided an in-depth lecture on the science and technology behind weather forecasting. He explained how meteorologists use various instruments and data to predict weather patterns and shared insights into the tools and techniques employed in their daily operations.

Additionally, Mr. Gonzales guided us through the procedures involved in airplane takeoffs and landings, illustrating the critical role of weather monitoring in aviation safety. His expertise and enthusiasm made the learning experience both informative and engaging.

Mr. Dave Del Rosario

During our visit to the First Aviation Academy, Sir Dave gave us a detailed tour. We explored the aircraft under repair and learned about their training methods, emphasizing the impactful role of technology. The highlight was their advanced flight simulator, which allows students to safely practice flying. This experience highlighted the academy's commitment to integrating technology into aviation education, ensuring both safety and effective learning.

Engr. Jerome Saddi

During our visit, Engr. Jerome Saddi guided us through the ship management system, showcasing the radar, installed cameras, and other essential equipment. He explained how these tools are crucial for identifying illegal activities, although he didn't provide detailed navigation training. We were also invited to visit the signal tower, where we could observe cargo ships sailing in the bay and get a closer look at the operations from a higher vantage point.

Ms. Karla Legaspi

When we stopped at QBO Innovation Hub, Ms. Karla Legaspi shared some crucial insights about overcoming challenges in our careers and personal lives. I learned from her that instead of avoiding problems and forgetting them, we should embrace them to find suitable solutions or even better ones. It was a real eye-opener for me to hear and experience the wisdom she shared with us during that time.

Ms. Rose Anne Caraan

On the second day of our visit, we observed the workings of Pocketworkx Inc. IoT Technologies Hub in the afternoon. Ms. Rose Anne Caraan guided us through an eye-opening experience, showcasing a future where everyday objects are interconnected through the internet. We noted how the company is dedicated to innovating and developing ideas that aim to make human lives much easier. Her explanations highlighted the potential impact of their work on our daily lives.

Mr. Adrian Francisco Salda

During this visit, under the guidance of Mr. Adrian Francisco Salda, I had the unique opportunity to ride a train alongside our professors. Initially, I expected a rough ride, but as soon as the train started moving, I was pleasantly surprised by how smooth and effortless it felt. Mr. Brian Tanseng

At 98 Labs, Mr. Brian Tanseng shared the company's journey from a small startup to a prominent player in software development for major banks in the Philippines. Primarily focused on developing software and web applications, 98 Labs has built a notable clientele in the banking sector. It was fascinating to learn how they have successfully scaled up and delivered innovative solutions tailored to the financial industry's needs.

Ms. Gretchel Listana

During our visit, Ms. Gretchel Listana guided us as we observed and engaged with robots firsthand. Students were actively programming these robots to perform various tasks, showcasing their practical skills. We were even invited to use remote controls to interact with the robots ourselves. Witnessing how they integrate cutting-edge technology into their educational approach, under Ms. Listana's guidance, left a lasting impression on me.
